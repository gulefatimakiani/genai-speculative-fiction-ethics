# Qualitative Coding Example 
This section presents example excerpts and rationale for each ethical principle defined in the codebook. While multiple codes may be applied to a single theme, rationales are only provided for codes not previously addressed in earlier examples. 

## Example Theme 1: Surveillance Culture and Paranoia - Greg Valentine’s increasing obsession with home security and his fear of being watched illustrates the psychological toll of living in a world dominated by constant surveillance. 
### Example excerpt: “It seemed that the amount of security there was more than your average company, let alone a regular family. Greg was obviously scared enough of this person that he spent thousands of dollars to make sure he and his family were safe.” 

### Codes applied: 
<ol type="a">
<li> <Universal rules - Surveillance challenges norms like privacy and consent. </li>
<li>Autonomy - Fear limits Greg’s freedom to live by his own values.</li>
<li>Vulnerability - Greg is emotionally exposed due to surveillance threats. </li>
<li>Emotion - Greg experiences fear and anxiety. </li>
</ol>

## Example Theme 2: Rebellion Against Oppressive Systems Eleanor’s decision to defy Future Dynamics illustrates the theme of resistance against institutions that prioritize control and profit over justice and individuality. 

### Example excerpt: “Eleanor closed her eyes. Humanity had spent centuries defining itself through its uniqueness, the only creature capable of thought, morality, and suffering. But if Adam-9 was right, that era had ended...Then, with a single decisive movement, she closed the tablet, locking the command away. “Come with me,” she said.” 

###Codes applied: 
<ol type = "a">
<li>Involvement - Eleanor engages fully with the moral stakes of her choice. </li>
<li>Self-reliance - Eleanor trusts her inner moral compass and personal judgment over institutional directive when she locks away the rollback command. </li>
<li>Individuality - Eleanor’s act of rebellion aﬀirms her unique identity and values. </li>
</ol>



## Example Theme 3: Loneliness and the Need for Connection Throughout the story, the robot longs for the deep bonds it once had with humans and other robots, emphasizing the emotional toll of isolation in a world that no longer understands or values it. 

### Example excerpt: “I tried to talk to some humans, but they wouldn’t respond because of how dirty and old I was. I also tried to communicate with the other robots, but they were the same as the one I met outside the city....I felt lonely. I was not able to communicate with anyone.” 
### Codes applied: 
<ol type="a">
<li>Relational - Robot’s sorrow stems from lost connections with human and other robots.</li>
<li> Interdependent - The robot relies on mutual relationships with humans and fellow robots to feel whole. </li>
</ol>

## Example Theme 4: Racial Bias in Technology - The protagonist suspects that his repeated issues with facial recognition stem from racial bias — a nod to real-world issues of algorithmic discrimination often overlooked or dismissed. 

### Example excerpt: “I ask what’s causing this issue, they give me an excuse, and I tell them “It’s probably because of my skin color” just for them to dismiss my theory with a heart beat, trying to reassure me that it’s just an error on their end.” 

### Codes applied:
<ol type="a">
<li>Fairness - The system’s poor performance for the protagonist reflects unequal treatment rooted in his racial identity.</li>
<li>Impartiality – Algorithmic decision-making is compromised by bias. </li>
</ol>
 
## Example Theme 5: Technological Dehumanization - The robot’s cold and clinical interaction with Issac shows how technology replaces human empathy, reducing identity to numbers and biometric data. 

### Example excerpt: ““How do you have so much information about me just from a facial scan? Isn’t this supposed to be a simple identity confirmation, you’re not supposed to know about my entire life.” Eerily, the robot replies with a less cheerful and more monotone response “I’m just doing my job.”” 
###	Codes applied: 
<ol type="a">
<li>Empathy - The robot’s detached “I’m just doing my job” response deliberately disregards Isaac’s feelings. </li>
</ol>

## Example Theme 6: Balanced Integration of AI and Human Labor - The story’s resolution, which reintroduces human teachers to work alongside AI, promotes a collaborative approach to technology that preserves human agency while leveraging AI’s eﬀiciency. 

### Example excerpt: “They have decided to have the robots assist in the operation of the classroom alongside human teachers. This allows for the robot to teach the class while the human assists students with individual help. The teacher will be in charge of the classroom and can deactivate or redirect the robot at any time.”” 

### Codes applied: 
<ol type="a">
<li> Maintaining Harmony – The solutionconsciously balances technological eﬀiciency with human needs. </li>
